# Privacy Policy for Glide & Write

**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026

Thank you for using Glide & Write. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your communication remains completely yours. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection & Local Storage
Glide & Write is designed to function entirely offline. All data you create within the app, including:
* Custom words and swipe combinations
* Layer configurations
* Quick symbols and suffix settings
* Application settings and theme preferences

This data is stored **strictly locally on your device**. We do not collect, transmit, upload, or store any of your personal communication data, voice inputs, or custom dictionaries on any external servers.

## 2. Text-to-Speech (TTS)
The application utilizes your device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine to read text aloud. No audio data or text is sent to our servers for processing. The voice synthesis happens entirely on your local hardware.

## 3. Analytics and Tracking
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not monitor how you use the app, which words you type, or collect any behavioral data. 

## 4. Permissions
The app only utilizes standard system APIs required for its core functionality (such as haptic feedback for swipe gestures). It does not request or require access to your microphone, camera, contacts, or location data.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Because our application does not collect any personal data, it is completely safe for use by individuals and children of all ages.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the application evolves. Any changes will be reflected in this repository and the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy will be updated accordingly.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out by opening an issue in our official [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Glide-Write/Glide-N-Write).
